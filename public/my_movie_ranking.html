The Best Movies According to Flav

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Prestige

The best movie for me perfectly embodies all the flaws, temperaments, and vices of humanity.

Pursuit of Happyness

It competes very well with the movie 'The Prestige' for the best film category; this film reminds you that in life, some events/obstacles are not as difficult as we make them out to be in our minds.

Shutter Island

A suspense-filled movie, with Leonardo DiCaprio and Mark Ruffalo delivering impeccable performances; the ending is the highlight of the entire film.
